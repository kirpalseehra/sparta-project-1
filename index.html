How to play:


First, decide randomly which player will be the beginner; moves are made alternatively,one by turn.


Player one must then click the column they wish to place their first counter. Player two then has their turn to do the same.


The winner is the first player who gets four of their own counters in a vertical, horizontal or diagonal row with no gaps between them.


Click 'New Game' to clear the board and play again.
